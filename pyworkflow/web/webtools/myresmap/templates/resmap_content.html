  • Upload and import your volume file by clicking on the import volumes box. Finally press "Execute"!

  • Click on the scipion - create 3d mask box.


    Customize the parameters to create the mask. Note that for monores, the mask must contain only signal, excluding noise. Once you are finished press "Execute"!

  • Click on the resmap - local resolution box.


    ResMap is software tool for computing the local resolution of 3D density maps studied in structural biology, primarily by cryo-electron microscopy (cryo-EM). Visit resmap for help.

  • monores is a fully automatic method to determine the local resolution of electron microscopy maps.

    Note: Some volumes are inside a spherical mask, if this is the case, please set "Is the original premasked?" to yes and provide the radius of the spherical mask.

    Visit monores - wiki for a deep help.

  • Blocres must use 2 half maps. You must import the 2 halves and then choose them separetely in "Half 1" and "Half 2" parameters.

  • Once a method has finished, click on its box to show the Summary of Inputs and Outputs.

    Click on the Analyze Results button to further analyze the results of each method.

    For the resmap method, the resmap output charts will be accessible and can be downloaded using the download button. .
