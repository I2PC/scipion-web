My local resolution

Use this tool to calculate the local resolution of your map by all three currently available methods in the field: The well known ResMap, Blocres (from Bsoft), and the newly introduced MonoRes (Xmipp - local resolution). Note that ResMap and Monores allow the calculation of local resolution from either the final map or two half maps, while Blocress requires two half maps.
