My movie alignment

To align movies obtained in an electron microscope with a Direct Electron Detection device (DED) we provide both Li et al. 2013 approach, to correct for global frame movements, as well as Abrishami et al. 2015, Optical Flow method, allowing for local correction of within-frame movements. The two methods can be applied independently on in a combined fashion. At the end of the processing you will be able to check the between-frames global movements, the degree of local movement in each frame, as well as to download the corrected averaged micrographs.
